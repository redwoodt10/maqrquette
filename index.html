About Our Party

Rooted in Marquette County.
Working for Every Community.

The Marquette County Republican Party has been the voice of conservative, common-sense governance in Michigan's Upper Peninsula for generations. We organize at the precinct level, support Republican candidates from local office to the statehouse, and create opportunities for every citizen to make a difference.

Whether you're a lifelong Republican, a first-time voter, or a community leader looking for a seat at the table, we're here to listen, organize, and act. Our county party is active year-round — not just during election season.
